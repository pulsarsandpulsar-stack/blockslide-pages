## How to play

1. **Drag** any block left, right, up, or down.
2. **Slide** — if the cell next to it is empty, the block slides until it hits another block or the wall.
3. **Swap** — if the cell next to it has another block, the two switch places.
4. **Match** 3 or more of the same color in a row or column to clear them and score points.
5. **Chain** — when clears trigger more matches, you earn bonus points.
6. **Beat the tide** — new blocks rise from the bottom over time. Don't let the stack reach the top.

A full animated tutorial is available inside the app. Go to **Settings → How to Play → Replay Tutorial** to watch it any time.

## Difficulty modes

- **Easy** — The tide rises every 16 seconds, constant. Perfect for unwinding.
- **Normal** — The tide rises every 12 seconds, constant. Balanced for any session.
- **Difficult** — The tide starts every 12 seconds and accelerates to 4 seconds as your score climbs. For high-score chasers.

Difficulty changes apply on your **next New Game**, not mid-round.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Why won't my block slide?**
A: A block can only slide into empty cells in its row. If the cells next to it are filled, try dragging up or down to swap with a neighbor instead.

**Q: I dragged onto another block — what happened?**
A: That's a swap. The two blocks switched places. If they have the same color, the swap is skipped (it would have been a wasted move).

**Q: How are points scored?**
A: 10 points per cleared block, plus chain multipliers (×1.5 per cascade), plus big-clear bonuses (NICE for 4 blocks, GREAT for 5, BIG CLEAR! for 6+).

**Q: How do I pause?**
A: Tap the pause icon in the top-right of the screen, next to the restart and settings buttons. The game also pauses automatically when you switch apps or open Settings.

**Q: How do I turn off sound or haptics?**
A: Open **Settings → Feedback** and toggle Sound, Haptics, or Screen Shake. All three are independent.

**Q: How do I switch to dark mode?**
A: Open **Settings → Theme** and pick Auto, Light, or Dark.

**Q: Where is my high score saved?**
A: Your best score is stored locally on your device. It is not synced across devices and does not travel with your Apple ID. If you delete the app, the score is removed.

**Q: Are there ads?**
A: No. Block Slide is completely ad-free.

**Q: Are there in-app purchases?**
A: No. The full game is included in the free download.

**Q: Does the app work offline?**
A: Yes — the app never needs a network connection.


**Q: The game froze / crashed / behaved unexpectedly.**
A: Please email us with the following information and we'll investigate:
- Your device model (e.g., iPhone 15 Pro)
- Your iOS version (Settings → General → About → Software Version)
- A short description of what happened
- A screen recording if possible
