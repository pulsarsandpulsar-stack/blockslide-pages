# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** June 12, 2026
**App:** Block Slide: Match Puzzle
**Developer:** Harpreet

---

## Summary

**We do not collect any personal data.**

Block Slide: Match Puzzle is a fully offline puzzle game. It does not contain analytics, advertising, tracking SDKs, social logins, or any code that transmits information about you to us or to any third party. We have no servers and we receive no data from the app.

---

## What stays on your device

The app stores the following items locally on your device using Apple's standard `UserDefaults` mechanism:

- Your best score
- Your selected difficulty (Easy, Normal, Difficult)
- Your selected theme (Auto, Light, Dark)
- Your sound, haptics, and screen-shake preferences
- A flag indicating whether you've completed the tutorial

This information never leaves your device. It is not synced, not uploaded, and not visible to us. If you delete the app, this information is removed from your device by iOS.

---

## What we do not do

- We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or contact information.
- We do not collect device identifiers (such as IDFA, IDFV, or advertising IDs).
- We do not collect location data.
- We do not access your photos, camera, microphone, contacts, or any other system-protected resource.
- We do not show ads.
- We do not use analytics or crash-reporting services.
- We do not use third-party SDKs of any kind.
- We do not track you across other apps or websites.

---

## Children's Privacy

Block Slide: Match Puzzle is rated **4+** and is safe for children. We do not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app complies with the U.S. Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) by design — because no data is collected.

---

## Your Rights (GDPR, CCPA, and others)

Privacy laws in jurisdictions including the European Union (GDPR), California (CCPA/CPRA), the United Kingdom, Canada, Brazil (LGPD), and elsewhere give users the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of their personal data.

Because we do not collect or process any personal data, there is no data for us to access, correct, delete, or share. If you would like to remove the locally-stored preferences listed above, you may do so at any time by deleting the app from your device.

## App Store

When you download the app from the Apple App Store, Apple may collect information about the transaction in accordance with Apple's own privacy policy. We do not receive any of this information beyond the anonymized download counts that Apple shares with developers. You can read Apple's privacy policy at <https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/>.

## Changes to this policy

We may update this policy if we add new features or if legal requirements change. If we make a material change, we will update the **Effective date** at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after a change indicates acceptance of the updated policy. We will never start collecting personal data without first updating this policy.
